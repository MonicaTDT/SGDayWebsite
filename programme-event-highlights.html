Event Highlights

Enjoy a fun-filled Singapore Day 2019 with your family and friends. Our reunion starts from 10.30am and does not end until 5.00pm.

Be prepared to immerse yourself in familiar taste, sight, touch, smell and sound from home. Taste the familiar flavours of home with Authentic Hawker Fare, be entertained as home-grown artistes take the stage in our "Made in Singapore" Concert with music, comedy, and more and take a tour around the Singapore Showcase to re-discover and be updated with the latest developments from Singapore.

Join us at Singapore Day 2019 and you stand a chance to win a pair of return Singapore Airlines tickets. Register before 28 Feb 2019 to triple your chances.
